Enrollment Requirements

  • Completed Enrollment Application Form
  • Proof of Residence
  • Copy of Birth Certificate
  • Previous School Records (if applicable)
  • Jamb Result (Optional)

Application Process

  1. Step 1:Download the Application form Online.
  2. Step 2: Print and fill the form
  3. Step 3: Gather all necessary documents.
  4. Step 4: Submit the application and documents in person at our admissions office.
  5. Step 5: Schedule an entrance examinations with our admissions team.
  6. Step 6: Await the decision notification via email.
