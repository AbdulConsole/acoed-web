About Adeshina College of Education, Share

In 2007, the idea of establishing a college of Technology was conceived and Hilltop school of Management and Technology was registered. However, many factors mitigated against the successful running of the programme, and that brought about unwanted set back to the college.

Owing to the fact that the needed structures were already available, the proprietor, determined to begin an educational institution, obtained approval to run Adeshina International College in the year 2009 to utilize the available resources.

The college was granted approval by Kwara State Ministry of Education and Technology on 3rd August, 2009. The approval to write WAEC and NECO Examination was received in the year 2014 and the first examination was written in the year 2015.

Adeshina International College Share is a fully tuition free College with students paying termly fee of #3000 only for registration.
